To perform a hard reset on a Kindle, press and hold the power button until the Power menu appears, then tap Restart. If the screen goes blank, continue holding the button for at least 40 seconds, and the device should reset automatically. A factory reset, which erases all data, can be initiated through the device's settings or by creating a file named DO_FACTORY_RESTORE on your computer and connecting it to your Kindle.
